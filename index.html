Background & Skillset

Data-driven optimization for clean energy deployment

I translate complex atmospheric, geographic, and electrical data into structured financial and physical profiles. Working at the intersection of energy yield modeling, database management, and grid integration, I support developer project financing, utility resource planning, and corporate ESG transactions. My focus is on reducing the Levelized Cost of Energy (LCOE) and optimizing battery charging dispatch regimes to build stable, grid-compliant, and carbon-negative systems.
